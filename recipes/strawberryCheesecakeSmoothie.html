Strawberry Cheesecake Smoothie

Description

This Strawberry Cheesecake Smoothie tastes like dessert but is made with wholesome, budget-friendly ingredients like frozen fruit and cottage cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 1/4 frozen strawberries
  • 1/4 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients. Prepare half a batch of Bethâ€™s Homemade Whipped Cream (using the heavy whipping cream, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract), and put it in the freezer to chill while you make your smoothie.
  2. Add banana, strawberries, cottage cheese, milk, and vanilla extract to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Top with homemade whipped cream and crumbled vanilla sandwich cookies.
  5. Enjoy!
